Unstable weather patterns influenced by climate change can lead to devastating sudden frost events which can be catastrophic for growers.

Frost Armour is a spray on solution designed to protect crops from frost damage during the spring growing season.

Features

Spray-on solution

Frost Armour is applied using standard spraying equipment.

Organic

Frost Armour is certified organic.

Lasts for 5 days

Frost Armour is applied in anticipation of a frost event. A single application can protect plants for up to 5 days.

Environmentally-friendly

Upon its removal, Frost Armour can be degraded into a fertilizer that further benefits plant growth.
