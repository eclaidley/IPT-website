Consulting Services

Innovative Protein Technologies is pleased to provide protein production and protein expression consulting services.

Plasmid Constructs

Our team has unique experience with the design and construction of these plasmids for almost any cellular production and purification method.

Production Systems

We can quickly and efficiently test your protein of interest in a variety of different expression systems, and determine which will produce the optimal yield of your active protein.

Expression Optimization

By testing a wide variety of induction temperatures, inducer concentrations, growth times, growth densities, and other factors, we can increase your protein yield up to 10x, while still maintaining function and folding.

Stability Testing

We are capable of performing both short and long-term testing on protein stability under a variety of pH, temperature, and storage conditions.

Functional Engineering

We have many options for post-production modifications, including tag removal, labeling, and activation. Proteins can be produced with requested tags for purification.
