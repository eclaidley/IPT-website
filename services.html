Consulting Services

Innovative Protein Technologies is pleased to provide protein expression consulting services. We can offer detailed experimental reports in any of the categories below.

Production Systems

We can quickly and efficiently test your protein of interest in a variety of different expression systems, and determine which will produce the optimal yield of your active protein. We offer detailed reports on the best production system for your protein with scale-up estimates.

Expression Optimization

By testing a wide variety of induction temperatures, inducer concentrations, growth times, growth densities, and other factors, we can increase your protein yield up to 10x, while still maintaining function and folding.

Stability Testing

We are capable of performing both short and long-term testing on protein stability under a variety of pH, temperature, and storage conditions. We will provide a full report on the stability of your protein, as well as recommended storage and buffer conditions for optimal solubility and protein lifetime.

Functional Engineering

We have many options for post-production modifications, including tag removal, labeling, and activation.

Protein Production Services

Innovative Protein Technologies is pleased to provide custom protein production services. We can offer detailed experimental reports in any of the categories below.

Protein Production

Our production team has unique experience focused on handling the production of medically and agriculturally relevant proteins toxic to normal production systems. We are highly experienced in E. coli and yeast (including S. cerevisiae and P. pastoris) production systems.

Protein Modification

Proteins can be produced with a 6xHis, GST, or FLAG tag for purification.

Plasmid Constructs

Our team has unique experience with the design and construction of these plasmids for almost any cellular production and purification method.
